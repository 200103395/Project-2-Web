National sports
Baiga - equestrian sport

Zhorga-zharys - horse racing
Kyz kuu (girl chase) - horse chase between a girl and a guy

Tenge alu - raise a coin at a gallop and other trick
Sais - horseback wrestling

Transport in Kazakhstan

Transport in Kazakhstan plays an important role. The vast territories of the country (2.7 million km²), low population density, disunity of the centers of industry and agriculture, as well as remoteness from world markets make the possession of a developed transport system vital for Kazakhstan.

Railway transport Kazakhstan railways provide 68% of the total cargo turnover and over 57% of the country's passenger traffic. The length of railways in Kazakhstan exceeds 15 thousand km.
Automobile transport In Kazakhstan, registered with the Ministry of Internal Affairs in 2020. consists of 4 million 520 thousand vehicles of all types. Kazakhstan has more than 95,629 km of road network for 2019
Air Transport There are 22 large airports in Kazakhstan, of which 14 serve international traffic.
Water transport The length of the waterways of Kazakhstan, open for navigation, is 3982 km.
